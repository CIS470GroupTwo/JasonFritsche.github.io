About Me

Hello, my name is Jason Fritsche. I am a student, veteran, and father. I was born and raised in Conroe, Texas. When I was 19 I decided to join the Air Force. I'm so glad now because I have my GI Bill and am able to use that to attend school. I served for seven years in the US Air Force. I was fortunate to have a couple of amazing assignments. I spent my first three years at Ramstein Air Base, Germany. If that wasn't awesome enough , I spent the following three years assigned to Aviano Air Base, Italy. I spent my last year in the Air Force at Little Rock Air Force Base, Arkansas. While in Little Rock I decided it was time to separate from Air Force and move back to Texas. I enjoyed my time serving our country but am glad I decided to get a taste of civilian life. Since I've been out I started going to school full time at DeVry University.
